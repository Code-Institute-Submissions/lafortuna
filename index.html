"Mi casa es tu casa"

It all began in February 2014, when a young Spaniard decided to buy a small van and start selling churros with hot chocolate to help fight the cold winter of Edinburgh.
Only a year later, after a very successful summer traveling from coast to coast serving at festivals, private events, concerts and even one 50th birthday party... and some other events that were missed due to being such a one man band back then, the business needed to expand not only on number of staff, but also on size. The "Wee Churros Van", as it was popularly known, became "La Fortuna Food Truck".
"La Fortuna" not only means "fortune, luck, wealth" in Spanish, it is also the place where this Spaniard was born and bred, a small town on the outskirts of the big capital that is Madrid. The decission of using this name has kind of both meanings, an homage to my hometown, and also as a sign of good luck.
And it seems to have worked, within the last few years the success of the food truck kept growing and growing, offering not only churros, but also some cold and hot tapas dishes that were going down a treat on corporate events, weddings, aniversaries, galas... and even the world famous Edinburgh Fringe!
Such was the interest shown on Spanish food, that we decided to take a leap of faith and open the restaurant on the summer of 2017. It started having a small tapas menu and some daily specials... now it has a full menu serving a wide range of different traditional Spanish dishes (some of them with a twist) that are sure to please all tastes.
But the story does not end here yet. We are very fortunate to announce the latest addition to the "La Fortuna Family"... our very own catering service, able to cater for up to 150 people.
You can find our more of what we can do on the specific pages for our restaurant, food truck and catering services on this website on getting in touch with us via the info at the bottom of the page... or just pop into our restaurant and let's talk face to face... the Spanish way.
