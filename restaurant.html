"El Restaurante"

Located at the very popular Fort Kinnaird Shopping Mall, it is ideal for having some lunch ahead of an afternoon of shopping or to relax with a nice dinner after a day of retail therapy or before a night at the movies. The choice is yours!
With 27 tables distributed across the open plan area, the restaurant offers a relaxed atmosphere, with low lighting in the evenings letting the lit candles create the perfect ambience for a romantic meal or offering a quite time after a day at work.
The whole room is decorated with a mix of old and modern photographs and pictures of iconic Spanish landmarks, monuments, landscapes, folklore and famous figures, inmersing you in the diverse culture and tradition of Spain and its different regions. It all is accompanied with some background Spanish music from all genres, old and new, making you feel like if you were in Spain... and all of it without having to go on a plane.
The "A la Carta" menu offers a wide range of different tapas options for all tastes and likes, including fresh fish and seafood, vegetarian options and the most typical meat dishes. All the tapas in this menu are a mixture of pure traditinal recipes and some new innovations and twists on the "Grandmother's recipes". On this menu you will find from all time favourites like Tortilla Española (Spanish Omelette) or Patatas Bravas (Spicy Potatoes) to some probably unknown and different dishes such as Paella Negra (Black Paella) or Tajine de Cordero (Slow-cooked Lamb Shoulder).
Another option is our "Menú del Día (Daily Menu), which changes daily and offers a smaller selection of dishes, but a bread basket, water for the table and coffee are included on the price, giving more value to your meal. You can even add a dessert for just an extra £5!
